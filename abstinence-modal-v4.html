ABSTINENCE MODAL — FLOW

"Yes" should NOT auto-post. Users need to see what they're sharing before confirming.

The Principle

1
User sees question + optional media on one screen
2
They can add photo/note before confirming (or skip it)
3
One button logs the answer + posts whatever they attached
4
No auto-posting. Nothing goes out until they tap confirm.
